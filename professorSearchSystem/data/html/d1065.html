Zvi Galil

Julian Clarence Levi Professor of Mathematical Methods and Computer Science at the Computer Science Department of Columbia University in the city of New York. He currently serves as the Morris A. and Alma Schapiro Professor and Dean of the School of Engineering and Applied Science at Columbia University. His main research interests are in the design and analysis of algorithms, computational complexity and cryptography.
