About

Hi! I’m Shan Jiang (江山).

I study (mis)information as an ecosystem by observing the behaviors of its players. My work on this topic includes: understanding audiences’ responses on social media (CSCW’18a, ICWSM’20), auditing platforms’ interventions such as content moderation (ICWSM’19, AAAI’20) and search ranking (CSCW’18b), structurizing fact-checkers’ articles for search engines (WWW’20), etc.

More broadly, I’m interested in (mostly) computational social science and (recently) natural language processing. Related work can be found in my publications.
