Alexa VanHattum

About

I am a second-year PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at Cornell University. I work with Adrian Sampson in the Computer Architecture and Programming Abstractions research group. My research interests span systems programming languages, applied formal methods, and usability for programming tools. I completed my bachelor’s at Brown, where I worked on some projects using modeling tools and refinement types. I then spent 2 years engineering health software at Apple.
