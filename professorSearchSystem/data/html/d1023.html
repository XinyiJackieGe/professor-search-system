A short overview of my life:


Seattle, WA, USA
where I currently work as an assistant professor in the Paul G. Allen School of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Washington

Ann Arbor, MI, USA
where I was an assistant professor in the School of Information at the University of Michigan

Cambridge, MA, USA
where I worked as a postdoc with Krzysztof Gajos at the Harvard School of Engineering and Applied Sciences

Zurich, Switzerland
where I did my PhD in the Dynamic and Distributed Information Systems Group under the supervision of Abraham Bernstein and Anthony Jameson

Kigali, Rwanda
where I developed an e-learning platform for the Projet Agricole et Social Interuniversitaire

Butare, Rwanda
where I did research for the Projet Agricole et Social Interuniversitaire and for my diploma thesis

Koblenz, Germany
where I studied computational visualistics and spent much of my free time writing articles for the Service Civil International in nearby Bonn

Rostock, Germany
where I spent my last two years of high school and enjoyed living a stone's throw away from the Baltic sea

Nathalia, Australia
where I spent a year in high school

Hamburg, Germany
where I was born and grew up and which is probably the place I would call home.
