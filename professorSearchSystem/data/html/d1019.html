Welcome to my website! I am Jungo Kasai, a PhD student in Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Washington advised by Professor Noah A. Smith.

Prior to UW, I received a B.S. in Statistics at Yale University, advised by Professor Robert Frank and Dr. Owen Rambow from Columbia. I also worked on projects with Professor Dragomir Radev in the Department of Computer Science. I was a member of the LILY Lab and the CLAY Lab.
My research interests include text generation, multilingual NLP, structured prediction, and neural networks.
