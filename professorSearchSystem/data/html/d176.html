Chin Ho Lee

Email: c.h.lee (at) columbia edu

I am a postdoc at the Computer Science Department of Columbia University. I completed my PhD at Khoury College of Computer Sciences of Northeastern University, advised by Emanuele Viola. I did my Master's in Computer Science and Engineering (advised by Andrej Bogdanov) and Bachelor's in Mathematics at the Chinese University of Hong Kong.

I visited Amnon Ta-Shma at Tel Aviv University in Summer and Fall of 2018. I was also a visiting graduate student at the Simons Institute in UC Berkeley in 2015 Fall, and Harvard University during the year of 2014-2015.
