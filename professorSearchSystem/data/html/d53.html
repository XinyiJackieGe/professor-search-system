About me

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science@Northeastern University advised by Prof. Guevara Noubir . I am interested in solving practical problems of security and networked systems, broadly defined. Previously, I got my B.E. on Electronics and Communication Engineering at Hanoi University of Science and Technology (HUST) in June 2016 in the highest honor program. At HUST, I had worked with Prof. Thang Truong (the University of Aizu, Japan) and Prof. Nam Pham (HUST) on problems of Quality-of-Experience assurance in multimedia streaming.
