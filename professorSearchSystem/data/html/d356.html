DATA lab @ Northeastern

My research interests are in the area of data and information management. One of my goals is to extend the capabilities of modern data management systems in generic ways and to allow them to support novel functionalities that seem hard at first. Examples of such functionalities are managing provenance, trust, explanations, and inconsistent data. I am currently particularly interested in developing scalable approaches to perform inference over uncertain and networked data, for which am very gratefully supported by NSF Career Award 1762268 (formerly 1553547).
