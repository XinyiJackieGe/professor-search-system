Integrated Photonics Laboratory

 

Welcome to the Integrated Photonics Laboratory (IPL)! We are a research group led by Professor Ming C. Wu in the Electrical Engineering and Computer Sciences (EECS) department at UC Berkeley. Our projects cover areas in Silicon photonics, optoelectronics, nanophotonics, optical MEMS, and optofluidics. Please feel free to contact us for further information.
