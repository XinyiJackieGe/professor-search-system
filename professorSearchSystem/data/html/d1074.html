Since January 2019 I'm a Senior Cryptography Engineer at Findora. Previously, I was a Research and Development Engineer at Dreamlab Technologies AG. Under the advise the Professor Tal Malkin, on October 2015 I defended my PhD thesis "Secure Computation Towards Practical Applications", where I focused on sublinear secure two-party computation and private search mechanisms. My main areas of interest are theoretical and applied cryptography.
