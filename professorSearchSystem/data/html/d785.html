Prasad Raghavendra

Computer Science Division,

I received my Phd from Computer Science and Engineering Department at University of Washington, Seattle, advised by Venkatesan Guruswami. I then spent a year Microsoft Research New England as a postdoc. Earlier, I got my Dual degree(Btech/Mtech) in computer science from IIT Madras.

Research Interests: Approximation Algorithms, Hardness of Approximation, Complexity, Coding theory.
