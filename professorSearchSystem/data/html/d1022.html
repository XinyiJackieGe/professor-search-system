I am an Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Washington. I also lead the PRIOR team at the Allen Institute for Artificial Intelligence. I am mainly interested in computer vision, machine learning, the intersection of natural language and vision, analysis of the role of semantics in visual understanding, and visual reasoning.
