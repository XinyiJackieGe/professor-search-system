    Research 

My primary research interests are in Secure Machine Learning, ,Datacenters, mobile/distributed computing, and wireless communications (networking and telephony). I am part of the AMP Lab and am exploring these areas in current (Mesos, SecML, DETER, and SCRUB) and former (RAD Lab, D-Trigger, Tapas, Tapestry, Reap, Sahara, Ninaja, and Iceberg) projects.
