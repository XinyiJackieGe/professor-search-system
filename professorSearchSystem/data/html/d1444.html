Chenfanfu Jiang received his Ph.D in CS from UCLA in 2015, co-advised by Prof. Demetri Terzopoulos and Prof. Joseph Teran. He was awarded UCLA Edward K. Rice Outstanding Doctoral Student Award in 2015. He received his B.S. in Physics from Class for the Gifted Young (SCGY) of University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2010. His primary research focus is physics-based simulation for animation/VFX and its cross-field collaborational research with computer vision, scene understanding, robotics, cognitive science, and medical training.
