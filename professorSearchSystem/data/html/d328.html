About me

Hi there! I am a former entrepreneur,  system administrator  and nowadays a PhD student in Cybersecurity at Northeastern University, Boston, MA, USA.

My current research interests are related to Linux, IoT, Cyberphysical systems and system security. Specifically, I research testing methods (fuzzing and symbolic execution) for embedded devices. My preferred platforms are single-board computers (Raspberry PI, Beagle Bone Black/Green) and micro-controllers (Microchip/Atmel, ST, Nuvoton, Nordic Semiconductors, and NXP).
