    I am a Research Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer and Information Science at the University of Pennsylvania. I am also affiliated with the Department of Biomedical and Health Informatics at the Children's Hospital of Philadelphia. I am a member of the PRECISE Center. My research develops foundations and tools for safety guarantees and predictable performance of learning-enabled cyber-physical systems (CPS) - primarily targeting the internet-of-medical-things (IoMT) and autonomous vehicles (AVs). Specifically, my research aims to develop tools and techniques to address challenges arising from "thin data", which can be caused by unmodeled variability, dataset shifts, anomalies, and sensing constraints.
