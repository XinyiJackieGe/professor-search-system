Recent Updates

  • i. ☀️ (March 2020) Moses’ paper on revisiting the ortholog conjecture (OC) accepted (conditionally) for ISMB 2020. Also available on bioRxiv. The results confirm the original doubts in the OC and quantify the value of paralogs when predicting protein function.
  • ii. ☀️ (February 2020) We received a MathWorks micro-grant ($25,000) to improve our software for positive-unlabeled learning.
  • iii. ☀️ (February 2020) Richard’s paper, from Matt Hahn’s group, on estimating sequencing error rates available on bioRxiv. Distinct error rates observed on reference vs. non-reference genotypes.
  • iv. ☀️ (February 2020) Jay’s paper giving a citation analysis of computational biology conferences published in Bioinformatics. Available here.
