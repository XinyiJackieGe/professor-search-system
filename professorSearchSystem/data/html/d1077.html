Timothy Sun
About Me
I am currently a Visiting Assistant Professor of Computer Science at Emory University. I recently completed my PhD at Columbia University under the supervision of Xi Chen and Rocco Servedio. I'm currently interested in graph theory, especially that surrounding the Map Color Theorem, and property testing.

In my spare time, I like to solve Rubik's Cube and related puzzles for speed. I used to compete actively. I was part of the Columbia badminton club, serving as team captain from 2012-2019.
