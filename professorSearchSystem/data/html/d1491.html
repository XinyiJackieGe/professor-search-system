Lyle H. Ungar

Professor
Computer and Information Science (CIS)

Email | Personal Webpage

Honors and Awards:  NSF Presidential Young Investigator Award

Research Expertise: Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing, Psychology

Lyle's current research focuses on developing scalable machine learning methods for data mining and text mining, including deep learning methods for NLP, and analysis of text and images in social media to better understand the drivers of physical and mental well-being.
