About

I am a fourth year (2016 - present) PhD Student at the College of Computer and Information Science at Northeastern University. I study Natural Language Processing with David Smith. Previously, I studied Computer Science and Greek and Roman Studies at Rhodes College and conducted Virtual Environment research with Betsy Sanders.
